Uncertainty

Category: Belief Mechanics

Definition: Uncertainty describes the presence of unresolved questions, conflicting evidence, or incomplete understanding that prevents a belief from stabilizing.

What this is

  • A natural state in emerging or complex topics.
  • A signal of opportunity for scaffolding.

What this is not

  • Not ignorance.
  • Not failure or error.

Example signals

  • Signal: Conflicting explansations.
  • Signal: Frequent qualifiers.
  • Signal: Calls for further research or context.
