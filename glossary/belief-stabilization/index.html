Belief Stabilization

Category: Trust & Sources

Definition: Belief stabilization occurs when explanations become consistent, bounded, and widely accepted enough to be repeated with minimal hedging..

What this is

  • The end state of effective scaffolding.
  • The condition for confident synthesis.

What this is not

  • Not permanent
  • Not universal

Example signals

  • Signal: Reduced hedging.
  • Signal: Consistent language across outputs
